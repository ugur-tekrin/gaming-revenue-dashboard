# Gaming Subscription Analytics

A subscription revenue analytics project built with SQL and Tableau to analyze recurring revenue growth, paid user behavior, churn dynamics and revenue recovery patterns in a gaming environment.

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing subscription-based gaming revenue data using business intelligence and product analytics concepts.

The dashboard was designed to monitor:

- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Paid User Growth
- ARPPU (Average Revenue Per Paid User)
- Churn & Revenue Loss
- Revenue Recovery
- Expansion vs Contraction Revenue

The goal of the project is to better understand revenue drivers, retention risks and user monetization behavior.


## Key Business Metrics

- Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- Paid Users
- ARPPU
- New MRR
- Churn Rate
- Revenue Churn Rate
- Churned Revenue
- Expansion MRR
- Contraction MRR
- Revenue Recovery

## Key Insights

- Revenue growth accelerated during the second half of the year.
- Paid user growth directly impacted recurring revenue increase.
- Churn became one of the biggest revenue risk factors.
- Revenue recovery partially compensated churn losses.
- Expansion revenue remained positive while contraction pressure increased.
